## Messaging Do's and Don'ts

### DO ‚úÖ
- Emphasize computational rigor and academic validation
- Show real backtesting results with confidence intervals
- Explain ensemble architecture benefits
- Compare to institutional techniques
- Be transparent about risks and limitations
- Use technical terminology appropriately
- Cite academic papers (arXiv references)

### DON'T ‚ùå
- Promise guaranteed returns
- Use "get rich quick" language
- Hide methodology in black boxes
- Oversimplify complex algorithms
- Compare to retail "gurus" or signal sellers
- Use hype language ("revolutionary", "secret", "holy grail")
- Claim to "beat the market" without context
